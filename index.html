Overview

I've been waiting for the chance to have control over my own place for my whole life, and now it's finally time for me to get started. I'll be able to get my own furniture, arrange things however I want, and paint the walls any color I choose. I've been ready for this opportunity for so long, and I'm incredibly excited that the time is arriving!

If I'm going to do this, I want to do it right. I will collect images of ideas I want to try, gather resources full of affordable tips and tricks, and compile a list of as many apartments as I can find to sort through. Join me, dev bootcamp class, as I rise to the challenge of creating my new home!
